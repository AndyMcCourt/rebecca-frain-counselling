Welcome

Thank you for visiting Rebecca Frain Counselling. I believe therapy provides a safe and confidential space where you can talk to a trained professional about your issues and concerns. According to the British Association for Counselling and Psychotherapy (BACP), a therapist will help you explore your thoughts, feelings and behaviours so you can develop a better understanding of yourself and of others.

As a counsellor, my role is not to give you advice or prescribe medication, but to help you find your own solutions in a way that respects your individuality. Whether you are experiencing stress, anxiety, loss or relationship difficulties, I am here to support you on your journey toward greater wellbeing.

Why Choose Rebecca?

With professional training and a warm, empathetic approach, I prioritise creating a non‑judgemental environment where you can feel heard and understood. I am a member of the BACP and adhere to their ethical framework, ensuring that your wellbeing, autonomy and confidentiality are at the centre of our work together.

Clients appreciate my calm and welcoming manner, which helps them feel at ease as we explore challenges such as anxiety, low mood, self‑esteem or relationship issues. Sessions are tailored to your needs and usually last around 50 minutes.

Our Services

I offer one‑to‑one counselling for individuals as well as support for couples and families. Sessions can take place face‑to‑face in Lymm, online via video call or by telephone, providing flexibility and privacy. Visit the services page to learn more about how counselling can help you and what to expect in your sessions.
