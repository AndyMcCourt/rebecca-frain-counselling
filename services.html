Counselling Services

I offer a range of counselling services designed to support you in a way that fits your life. Sessions typically last around 50 minutes and take place at a pace that feels right for you. We will work collaboratively to understand your experiences and help you find a path forward.

Individual Counselling

One‑to‑one sessions provide a private space where you can explore personal challenges, thoughts and emotions. Whether you’re feeling overwhelmed by anxiety, coping with bereavement or wanting to understand patterns in your relationships, individual counselling offers a supportive environment to gain insight and develop coping strategies.

Relationship Counselling

Relationship or couples counselling helps partners work through difficulties together. Sessions provide a neutral space to improve communication, rebuild trust and understand each other’s needs more clearly. I also offer family sessions to support healthy dynamics and address conflicts.

Online & Telephone Counselling

For clients who prefer the flexibility of remote sessions or cannot attend in person, I provide counselling via secure video call or telephone. This can be particularly helpful if you have mobility difficulties, live outside the Lymm area or simply want to engage in therapy from the comfort of your home. Therapy can take place face to face, online or over the telephone and may involve specific exercises or more general discussions depending on your needs.
